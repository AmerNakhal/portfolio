Design process

A clear design workflow from understanding requirements to preparing clean, implementation-ready design deliverables.

Understand

Clarify business goals, user needs, product requirements, target screens, and expected user actions.

Structure

Create user flows, information architecture, screen maps, and low-fidelity wireframes.

Design

Build high-fidelity UI screens, design systems, responsive layouts, and interactive prototypes.

Deliver

Prepare organized design files, components, specs, and handoff materials for developers and clients.
